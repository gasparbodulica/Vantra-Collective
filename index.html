Vantra Collective is a two-sided matchmaking and campaign agency. Brands come to us with a goal — we find the creator on TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube whose audience already trusts them, and broker the partnership. Creators come to us to get in front of brands that are right for their audience and their content.

We specialise in micro and mid-tier creators — 10K to 500K followers. The tier the big agencies ignore, and the tier that consistently outperforms on engagement and ROI. Once the match is made, we run the full campaign and guarantee creators are paid within 7 days of going live.

One agency. Two sides. One campaign that actually converts.

Brands bring the brief. Creators bring the audience. We bring them together.

How It Works

Our approach,
in four steps.

Whether you're a brand looking for the right creator or a creator looking for the right brand deal — here's how a Vantra campaign works from first contact to final results.

01
Tell Us Who You Are

Brand? Tell us your product, audience, and goal. Creator? Tell us your niche, platforms, and what kind of brand you want to work with. That's all we need.

02
We Make the Match

We pair brands with creators whose audience already overlaps with their customers — and creators with brands that genuinely fit their content and values. No forced fits.

03
Campaign Goes Live

The creator publishes sponsored content to their audience. We handle the brief, creative direction, approvals, and posting — then boost the best-performing content with paid ads.

04
You Get the Results

Clear performance reporting after every campaign — reach, engagement, conversions, and a straight read on what to do next.

Services

What we offer.

We work with both sides — brands looking for the right creator, and creators looking for the right brand deal. Short-form or long-form, one market or ten. Click any service to learn more.

Short-Form Long-Form Global Multi-Platform
We run campaigns across the full content spectrum and the full global map. Short-form — TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts — drives fast discovery and rapid engagement. Long-form — YouTube reviews, podcast integrations, editorial features — builds deeper trust that lasts. Most campaigns benefit from both, and we match the format to the goal.

Globally, trend cycles don't respect borders and neither do we. Our creator network spans multiple markets — we adapt creator selection, language, and platform mix per region while keeping your brand message coherent. You brief us on the goal; we handle creator selection, briefing, content review, posting schedules, and performance tracking from start to finish.
We work both sides of the match. Brands come to us with a target audience — we find creators on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube whose followers already fit that profile. Creators come to us with their content and their niche — we connect them with brands that are a genuine fit for what they do.

Our matchmaking process goes beyond follower count: audience demographics, brand affinity, tone-of-voice alignment, and content performance history all factor in. The result is campaigns that feel native — not placed. For longer-term work, we build ongoing creator-brand relationships that compound over time, managing both sides so the partnership stays alive, on-brand, and performing.
Creator-made content consistently outperforms polished brand content in paid media. We source and license UGC from our network — short-form video, long-form reviews, photo, and editorial formats — that you can run as paid social ads, on your website, or in email campaigns. Clean licensing agreements, no rights headaches.
Physical moments drive digital amplification. We source and manage creators for brand events, product launches, pop-ups, and activations — ensuring real-time content across short and long-form formats, live audience reach, and post-event coverage that extends the moment well beyond the day itself.

We also run strategic gifting and seeding programs — identifying the right tier of creators globally, managing product delivery and follow-up, and tracking organic coverage. Getting product into the right hands before a paid campaign is one of the highest-ROI moves in creator marketing. We do it properly.

About

Built to move
with the market.

Most agencies pick a niche and stay there. We didn't.

Vantra Collective was built on a simple observation: trends move faster than the agencies built to capitalize on them. By the time a "lifestyle agency" or a "fitness-only roster" catches up to what's actually working, the moment has usually passed.

So we built something different — an agency structured around attention itself, not a fixed category. Our roster shifts with what's gaining traction, which means the brands we work with are never chasing last quarter's trend. They're already in it.

We started small — testing what kind of creator partnerships actually moved numbers, not just impressions — and built our process around staying close to the data instead of a fixed playbook. What began as a handful of partnerships has grown into a flexible, trend-responsive roster working across multiple markets.

We're still not interested in being "the fitness agency" or "the lifestyle agency." We're interested in being right about what's next.
